About EL HABAYEB

Welcome to EL HABAYEB, where food meets tradition and taste. Our restaurant offers a unique dining experience, combining the finest local ingredients with traditional recipes passed down through generations. We believe that food is more than just a meal – it's a celebration of culture, family, and good times.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 , EL HABAYEB began as a small family-owned restaurant in the heart of Borg EL-Arab City. Over the years, we’ve grown, but our commitment to providing fresh, flavorful meals with a touch of home has remained unchanged. Whether you're here for a quick bite or a special celebration, we strive to create an atmosphere where everyone feels like family.

Our Mission

At EL HABAYEB, we’re committed to using only the highest-quality ingredients to prepare every dish from scratch. We aim to offer a welcoming and relaxed environment where our guests can enjoy great food and good company. Our team is passionate about creating exceptional dining experiences, ensuring each visit is memorable.

What We Offer

  • Authentic Cuisine: Enjoy a wide variety of traditional dishes made with love.
  • Fresh Ingredients: We source the finest local produce, meats, and spices to deliver the best flavors.
  • Cozy Ambience: Whether you're enjoying a meal with friends or celebrating a special occasion, our warm atmosphere is the perfect setting.
  • Friendly Service: Our dedicated staff are here to make your dining experience unforgettable.

We hope to see you soon at EL HABAYEB – where every meal is a celebration!
